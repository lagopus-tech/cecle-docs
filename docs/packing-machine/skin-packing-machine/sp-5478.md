## 4. Operation Process
1. **Placement:** Put products on the cardboard and slide the board into the machine.
2. **Heating:** The ceramic plate heats the film to the ideal softening point.
3. **Vacuum Sealing:** Start the vacuuming; negative pressure pulls the film tight around the product and bonds it to the board.
4. **Collection:** After cooling, pull out the worktable and remove the finished package.

### FAQ
**Q1: Why is the power consumption 6KW?**
A1: To ensure the large 780*540mm ceramic plate heats evenly and reaches temperature quickly, 6KW is required for high stability during continuous work.

**Q2: How is ceramic heating better than lamp heating?**
A2: Ceramic plates provide more "stable" thermal radiation with no "dead spots." They also have better thermal inertia and a longer lifespan.

**Q3: Do I need an air compressor?**
A3: No, it relies on its internal powerful vacuum pump.

**Q4: Is it easy to move?**
A4: Yes, despite weighing 400KG, it has industrial wheels for workshop mobility.
