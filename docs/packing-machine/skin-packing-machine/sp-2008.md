## 4. Operation Process
1. **Setup:** Place products on the cardboard and push the tray into the machine.
2. **Heating:** Lower the frame; the system automatically softens the film.
3. **Vacuuming:** Activate the pump; negative pressure draws the film tight around the product.
4. **Finishing:** After cooling, open the frame and remove the sealed board for trimming.
