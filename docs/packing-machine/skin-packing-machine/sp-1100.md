## 4. Operation Process
1. **Preparation:** Neatly arrange products on the skin cardboard and place it on the 1100mm oversized worktable.
2. **Film Softening:** Lower the film frame; the lamp heating system uniformly heats the film until it is pliable.
3. **Vacuum Sealing:** Engage the vacuum pump; air is rapidly exhausted, causing the film to bond to the board and product.
4. **Collection:** After cooling, open the frame and remove the finished board for trimming.

### FAQ
**Q1: Why does this machine require 8KW of power?**
A1: Due to the massive heating area (over 0.6 m²), high power is necessary to ensure the lamps heat the entire surface quickly and uniformly for consistent quality.

**Q2: Is lamp heating slower than wire heating?**
A2: While slightly slower to preheat, lamp heating provides the "uniformity" essential for large-scale packaging, preventing film burn-through on such a wide area.

**Q3: Can the vacuum handle such a long table?**
A3: Absolutely. We use a powerful industrial vacuum pump that reaches the required negative pressure across the entire surface within seconds.

**Q4: Is it easy to move?**
A4: Despite weighing 416KG, it is equipped with heavy-duty industrial casters for easy mobility on flat workshop floors.
