> **Core Abstract**
> The Automatic Liquid Pre-made Pouch Packaging Machine (PBP-L Series) is a high-performance, automated solution engineered for precision packaging of liquids and viscous pastes such as juices, sauces, edible oils, and daily chemicals. Designed for food factories, chemical plants, and seasoning production lines, this equipment streamlines the process from bag feeding and coding to quantitative filling and heat sealing. Available in PBP-L200, PBP-L250, and PBP-L300 models to accommodate various pouch sizes, it features food-grade stainless steel construction, ensuring rigorous hygiene standards. With a filling accuracy of up to ±1% and operational speeds reaching 60 bags/minute, the PBP-L series delivers unmatched stability, significant material waste reduction, and enhanced production efficiency for modern high-frequency manufacturing environments.

### VI. Workflow
1. **Automatic Bag Feeding**: The robotic arm automatically picks up the pre-made bag and accurately transfers it into the clamps.
2. **Coding Identification**: Automatically prints the batch number or production date on the bag.
3. **Bag Opening Detection**: The bag mouth is opened via vacuum suction cups; the machine stops filling if no bag is detected.
4. **Precision Filling**: Liquid/paste is injected into the bag via a quantitative piston pump.
5. **Sealing Process**: Double heat-pressing sealing device ensures a tight, leak-free seal.
6. **Cooling & Discharge**: The heat-sealed edges are cooled and set, and the finished product is discharged stably via a conveyor belt.
