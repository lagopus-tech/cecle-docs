> **Core Abstract**
> The Automatic Powder Pre-made Pouch Packaging Machine (PBP-P series) is a robust, fully automated packaging solution engineered for high-precision handling of powdered materials such as milk powder, coffee, seasonings, and chemicals. 1. **Multi-Model Coverage**: Available in three specifications: PBP-P200/250/300 to meet production needs for different bag widths and capacities.
2. **Intelligent Detection System**: Features an "No bag, no fill; no bag, no seal" intelligent detection function, greatly reducing the waste of expensive packaging materials.

### VI. Workflow
1. **Automatic Bag Taking**: The robotic arm automatically picks up the pre-made bag from the storage area and transfers it to the clamps.
2. **Coding Processing**: Prints the production date or batch number.
3. **Bag Opening Detection**: The bag mouth is opened via vacuum suction cups; the system automatically detects, and if there is no bag or the bag is not opened, it will not fill.
4. **Precision Filling**: High-precision auger filling device delivers the quantitative powder into the bag.
5. **Heat Sealing**: The double heat-pressing sealing device completes the sealing of the bag mouth.
6. **Cooling & Discharge**: The seal is cooled and set, and the finished product is discharged smoothly via a conveyor belt.
