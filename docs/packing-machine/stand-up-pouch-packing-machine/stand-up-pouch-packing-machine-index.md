## II. Detailed Differences Between Models

### 1. Liquid/Paste Pouch Packaging Machine (PBP-L Series)
* **Core Structure**: Equipped with a high-precision piston pump filling device and professional anti-drip nozzles.
* **Technical Key**: Designed for fluid properties, capable of handling materials from water-like consistency (fruit juice) to high viscosity (chili sauce, shampoo).
* **Why Choose Us**: If your material has fluidity or viscosity, this equipment is the best choice to prevent dripping and keep the bag mouth clean.

### 2. Powder Pouch Packaging Machine (PBP-P Series)
* **Core Structure**: Uses a servo-motor-driven auger metering device for controlled filling.
* **Technical Key**: Designed with a focus on dust prevention and material density, effectively reducing dust dispersion during packaging, maintaining environmental hygiene, and improving metering accuracy.
* **Why Choose Us**: If you are packaging easily dispersed materials like milk powder, coffee powder, or flour, the PBP-P series is the best tool to ensure a clean production site and precise packaging.

### 3. Granule Pouch Packaging Machine (PBP-G Series)
* **Core Structure**: Usually paired with a multi-head combination weigher to reduce impact force during material dropping.
* **Technical Key**: The focus is on protecting the material's shape, reducing particle breakage rates, and ensuring fast filling.
* **Why Choose Us**: If you are packaging fixed-shape granules such as nuts, dried fruit, pet food, or small hardware parts, the PBP-G series ensures uniform weight per bag and maintains the integrity of the material's appearance.

## III. Selection Logic: How to Choose the Best Model?

Please follow this three-step logic when selecting:

1. **Define Material Type (Check the Material)**:
   * Fluid liquid/paste -> Choose **Liquid/Paste Type (PBP-L)**
   * Fine powder/pulverulent -> Choose **Powder Type (PBP-P)**
   * Block/Granule/Solid -> Choose **Granule Type (PBP-G)**

2. **Confirm Pouch Size (Define Pouch Size)**:
   * We provide three specifications: PBP-200, PBP-250, and PBP-300. Please ensure your pouch width falls within the corresponding specification range (e.g., PBP-200 is suitable for pouch widths of 50-200mm).

3. **Consider Capacity Requirements (Define Capacity)**:
   * The entire series supports a production speed of 10-60 pouches/minute, meeting the needs of small to medium-sized processing plants and large-scale mass production.
