### 1. Product Overview
This equipment features a **rotary multi-station design**, specifically engineered for high-efficiency preservation packaging of fresh meat, ready-to-eat meals, seafood, and deli products. By performing vacuum extraction followed by gas flushing (N2, CO2, O2), it significantly extends food shelf life while maintaining the product's original color, texture, and flavor.

* **High-Efficiency Production**: Rotary stations allow for simultaneous filling, sealing, and discharging, achieving speeds far exceeding traditional models.
* **Ultimate Preservation**: Gas replacement rate exceeds **99%**, with residual oxygen levels controllable between **0.5% - 1%**.
* **Space-Saving**: Compared to linear models, the footprint is reduced by approximately **30%**.

#### **2. Consumable Recommendations**
To ensure optimal sealing quality and preservation results, sealing film must be strictly matched to the tray material:
* **Film Specifications**: The maximum width of the sealing film used is **440 mm**. We recommend a standard film thickness of **80 microns**.
* **PP Trays**: We suggest using compatible PP sealing film.
* **PET or CPET Trays**: We recommend using **EVOH high-barrier sealing film**. Although EVOH film carries a higher cost, its superior oxygen barrier performance significantly enhances the shelf-life of the food.
