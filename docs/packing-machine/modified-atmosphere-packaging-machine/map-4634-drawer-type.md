#### **2. Consumable Recommendations**
To ensure optimal sealing quality and preservation results, the sealing film must be strictly matched to the tray material:
* **Film Specifications**: The machine supports a maximum film width of **440 mm**. We recommend a standard film thickness of **80 microns**.
* **PP Trays**: We suggest using compatible PP sealing film.
* **PET or CPET Trays**: We recommend upgrading to **EVOH high-barrier sealing film**. While EVOH film is priced higher, its superior oxygen barrier performance significantly extends the product's shelf life.

### 7. Workflow
1. **Manual Tray Placement**: Operator places the product-filled trays into the mold.
2. **Automatic Feeding**: The machine automatically pushes the mold into the working chamber.
3. **Vacuum & Gas Flushing**: The system automatically extracts air and fills the chamber with the pre-set mixture of preservation gas.
4. **Heat Sealing & Film Cutting**: Synchronized completion of heat sealing and trimming of excess film edges.
5. **Photoelectric Correction**: The sensor identifies the film mark to ensure the design is perfectly centered.
6. **Automatic Discharge**: After packaging, the mold is automatically pushed out.
7. **Manual Unloading**: Operator removes the finished product and repeats the cycle.
