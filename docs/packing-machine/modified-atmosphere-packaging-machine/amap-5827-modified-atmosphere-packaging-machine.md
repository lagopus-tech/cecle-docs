### 1. Solution Overview
This equipment is a high-output, fully automated packaging solution designed for medium to large-scale food processing enterprises. It utilizes **linear stepping transport**, allowing for seamless integration with automatic denesting, filling, and downstream labeling systems. Through precision Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) technology, it ensures products maintain their original color and taste with a shelf life of up to 10-21 days.

* **High Throughput**: Supports multi-row mold parallel operation, with a capacity of over 700 boxes per hour.
* **High Integration**: Open-architecture design for easy integration into existing automated production lines.
* **Precision Control**: Full servo-motor drive ensures smooth operation, preventing liquid splashes during high-speed movement.

### 3. Key Selling Points

* **Transmission System**: 
  Uses high-precision motor-controlled chain stepping for stable starting, stopping, and precise positioning. This eliminates the impact force associated with traditional pneumatic transport, protecting materials from splashing or sticking to walls.
* **Segmented Gas Replacement Technology**: 
  Combines high-efficiency vacuum extraction with a multi-point uniform gas flushing system to ensure that the protective gas is distributed evenly across large trays or multi-row molds.
* **Intelligent Photoelectric Alignment**: 
  Equipped with high-sensitivity sensors to ensure that even complex printed films are accurately centered on every tray.
* **Quick-Change Mold Design**: 
  Engineered for diverse production needs, the mold system supports quick installation and removal, significantly reducing changeover time and increasing overall factory efficiency.
