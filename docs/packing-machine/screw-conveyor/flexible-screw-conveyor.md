## III. Operation Process
1. **Deployment and Bending**: Push the grain suction machine to the operation point and lay out the flexible pipe, bending it freely according to the site environment.
2. **Power Testing**: Connect to the power supply and confirm the rotation direction.
3. **Multi-Scenario Suction**: Insert the suction inlet into the grain pile or material bin to begin suction.
4. **Rapid Cleaning and Shutdown**: After operation, let the equipment run empty for 1–2 minutes to discharge residual material, then disconnect the power and retract the soft pipe.
