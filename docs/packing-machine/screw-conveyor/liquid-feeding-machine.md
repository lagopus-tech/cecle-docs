## II. Core Advantages

* **Fully Pneumatic Drive, 100% Industrial Explosion-proof**: Operates entirely without electricity, providing extreme safety when transporting flammable and explosive liquids (such as alcohol, fragrances, and solvents).
* **Automated Extraction**: Automatically extracts liquid from deep drums, significantly reducing labor intensity and increasing overall production line efficiency.
* **Superior Viscosity Adaptability**: Specifically designed for high-viscosity liquids and pastes, it can easily extract thick materials like shampoo, honey, and face cream.
* **High-Grade Materials**: Both the liquid-contact parts and the housing are manufactured from 304/316 stainless steel, ensuring corrosion resistance, easy cleaning, and compliance with hygiene standards.
* **Precision Adjustment**: Equipped with an adjustment handwheel on the side of the machine, allowing for intuitive and real-time control of transport pressure or liquid flow rate.

---

## III. Operation Process

1. **Equipment Positioning**: Insert the extraction tube directly into the storage drum and move the machine next to the target container.
2. **Air Source Connection**: Connect to the factory compressed air line meeting the pressure requirements (0.4–0.8 MPa).
3. **Flow Adjustment**: Use the adjustment handwheel to pre-set the pressure and flow rate.
4. **Automated Transport**: Turn on the pneumatic switch, and the liquid will be steadily injected into the target hopper via the stainless steel tube and hose.
