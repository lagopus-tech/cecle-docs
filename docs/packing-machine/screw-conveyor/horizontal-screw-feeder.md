# Product Name: Horizontal Screw Feeder

### I. Product Overview

![Horizontal Screw Feeder Demo](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0010/4982/1242/files/17825465579792.png?v=1782546613)

* **Category**: Powder/Granule Conveying and Feeding Equipment
* **Applications**: Widely used in automated production lines for food processing (e.g., seasoning powder, flour), chemical raw materials, pharmaceutical intermediates, and plastic granules.
* **Core Function**: Utilizes the rotation of an internal screw shaft to horizontally convey material from the hopper to the next stage of the process, ensuring an automated, continuous, and stable feeding process.

### II. Core Advantages

1. **Extremely Uniform and Precise Feeding**: The constant volume pushed per revolution of the screw ensures that material enters the production line at a constant speed, guaranteeing consistent quality.
2. **Low-Profile Design**: Ergonomically designed to reduce the height for manual loading, lessening employee physical exertion and operational risks.
3. **Powerful Anti-Bridging and Arch-Breaking Capability**: The forced agitation generated by the rotation effectively prevents sticky or clumped materials from "bridging" at the hopper opening, ensuring continuous material supply.
4. **Simple Maintenance**: The equipment features an intuitive structure that is easy to disassemble and clean, significantly reducing downtime for maintenance.

### III. Operation Process

1. **Loading**: Manual or automatic equipment deposits material into the hopper.
2. **Start-up**: Activate the equipment, and the screw shaft begins to rotate at a constant speed.
3. **Conveying**: Material is moved horizontally along the pipe, driven by the forced propulsion of the screw blades.
4. **Discharging**: Material enters the next processing stage continuously and steadily.
5. **Maintenance**: Clean the hopper and screw channel after operation.
