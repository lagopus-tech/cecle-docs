## I. Product Overview
* **Category**: Industrial Automated Handling Equipment / Screw Elevator
* **Product Name**: Screw Conveyor (Auger Elevator)
* **Applicable Materials**: Powders (e.g., flour, medicine powder, chemical powder), small granules (e.g., plastic pellets, coffee beans, seasonings).
* **Core Function**: Utilizes a motor-driven screw shaft to rotate and elevate bulk materials from the hopper to packaging machines, filling machines, or storage tanks at a specified height.

---

## II. Core Advantages
1. **304 Stainless Steel (SUS304)**: All **material-contact parts are made of 304 stainless steel**, providing rust and corrosion resistance, fully compliant with food and pharmaceutical hygiene standards.
2. **Customizable Height**: In addition to standard specifications (1.9m / 2.15m / 2.5m), the lifting height can be **customized to meet actual customer requirements**, ensuring a precise match for various production lines.
3. **Intelligent Automatic Speed Adjustment**: Features a built-in **automatic speed control/frequency conversion function**, which flexibly adjusts the conveying frequency based on the load of downstream production equipment to achieve synchronized automated feeding.
4. **Superior Mobility (Caster Design)**: The base is equipped with **heavy-duty industrial casters with brakes**, allowing a single person to easily move the equipment and lock it in the designated position, facilitating factory layout adjustments.
5. **Optional Agitator**: The hopper can be **fitted with a forced agitation device** to effectively solve the problem of material clogging and bridging, ensuring continuous feeding.

---

## III. Operation Process
1. **Deployment and Securing**: Use the casters to push the machine to the packaging machine, then lock the wheel brakes to prevent displacement.
2. **Power Testing**: Connect to a 110V/220V power supply, confirm the rotation direction of the screw, and test the speed control knob.
3. **Material Filling**: Pour the material into the 304 stainless steel hopper. If an agitator is equipped, activate it simultaneously.
4. **Start Feeding**: The equipment begins operation, and the material is steadily lifted through the screw tube to the top discharge outlet.
5. **Rapid Cleaning**: After operation, empty the remaining material through the cleaning port at the bottom of the machine body for easy internal maintenance and cleaning.

    * **Q: Is the agitator mandatory?**
        * A: For powders prone to moisture absorption and clumping, such as flour or medicine powder, we recommend it; it is generally not required for granules.
    * **Q: Is the machine stable when moved?**
        * A: The wheels are equipped with industrial-grade foot brakes; locking them during operation ensures the machine is rock-solid.
    * **Q: Can this machine lift viscous materials?**
        * A: This equipment is primarily designed for powders and granules with good flowability; it is not suitable for viscous materials.
    * **Q: Do I need tools to clean the machine?**
        * A: The design considers convenience; most cleaning tasks can be completed quickly through the cleaning port without complex disassembly.
