## I. Product Overview

![Bucket Elevator](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0010/4982/1242/files/buket.png?v=1782544848)

* **Category**: Industrial Vertical Conveying Equipment / Elevating Feeder.
* **Applications**: Widely used in the food, chemical, agricultural, and hardware industries for vertically lifting granular, blocky, or small flake materials (e.g., candy, nuts, chips, pet food) from low levels to the top of packaging machines, hoppers, or weighing equipment.
* **Core Function**: Utilizes chains or belts to drive buckets, lifting materials from a bottom hopper to the top for automated discharge, achieving automated material height transfer.

## II. Core Advantages

1. **Easy-Clean Design**: The buckets are designed to be detachable, significantly facilitating daily cleaning and disinfection—especially suitable for the food industry with high hygiene requirements.
2. **Highly Flexible Customization**: We offer standard lifting heights and support non-standard height customization based on your actual production line layout.
3. **Variable Frequency Speed Control**: The lifting speed can be adjusted between 0–15m/min via frequency control to adapt to different production tempos.
4. **Sturdy and Durable**: Core components, including the PLC, motor, bearings, and gearbox, are selected from high-specification components, ensuring smooth overall machine operation.

## III. Operation Process

1. **Loading**: Dump materials into the bottom hopper or provide continuous feeding from upstream equipment.
2. **Start-up**: Start the machine via the control cabinet; the frequency converter drives the motor to run.
3. **Vertical Lifting**: Driven by the drive mechanism, the buckets steadily transport materials upward.
4. **Automated Discharge**: Upon reaching the top turning point, the buckets use gravity to precisely discharge materials into the downstream equipment's intake.
5. **Speed Adjustment**: Adjust frequency values on the control cabinet at any time according to feeding requirements to change the conveying speed.
