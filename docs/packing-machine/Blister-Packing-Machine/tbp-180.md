# TBP-180 Desktop Pneumatic Blister Packing Machine

## 1. Product Overview
The TBP-180 is a desktop pneumatic blister packing machine, specially designed for PVC blister + aluminum foil sealing of small items such as capsules, tablets, small hardware, and electronic components. It adopts cylinder pressing + digital display time control, featuring stable operation and simple operation. It is suitable for small-batch production, laboratories, stores, factories, and small foreign trade orders.

## 4. Operation Steps
1. Manually put capsules, tablets and other products into the PVC blister;
2. Place the PVC blister with products into the machine mold, then add the aluminum foil film;
3. Press the equipment switch, the machine will automatically press down to complete the sealing operation;
4. Trim the excess aluminum foil film on the edge of the blister with scissors to get a complete sealed blister package.
