# BP-180 Vertical Blister Packing Machine

## 1\. Product Overview

The BP-180 is a semi-automatic flat-plate aluminum foil PVC blister packing machine, specially designed for aluminum foil-PVC blister sealing packaging of small solid products such as capsules and tablets. It is a basic and cost-effective model. Adopting pneumatic drive and digital temperature control, the machine features a compact structure, simple operation and low maintenance cost. It is suitable for small and medium batch production and widely used in pharmaceutical, health care, electronic components and other industries.

## 4\. Operation Procedure

1. Manually put capsules, tablets and other products into PVC blisters.
2. Place the PVC blister with products into the machine mold, and then put a piece of aluminum foil.
3. Step on the foot switch, and the machine presses down for heat sealing.
4. Cut the blister and trim the edges with scissors or a cutting machine.
5. Obtain the finished sealed product.

A: BP-180 is the basic model without automatic aluminum foil feeding function, requiring manual placement of aluminum foil piece by piece; CP-210 is an upgraded version supporting automatic aluminum foil feeding, with higher efficiency and higher price.
