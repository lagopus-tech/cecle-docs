## 4. Operation Steps
1. Manually put capsules, tablets and other products into the PVC blister.
2. Place the PVC blister with products into the machine mold.
3. Press the button, the machine will automatically press down for sealing.
4. The machine automatically rewinds/unwinds the aluminum foil.
5. Cut the blister and trim the edges with scissors.
6. Get the finished sealed product. |
