## 4. Operation Process
1. Manually put capsules, tablets and other products into PVC blisters;
2. Place the loaded PVC blister into the machine mold, then add aluminum foil film;
3. Turn on the device switch and press down the manual lever to complete sealing;
4. Trim excess aluminum foil around the blister with a cutter or scissors to get a finished package.
