## 4. Operation Procedure
1. Manually put capsules, tablets and other products into PVC blisters.
2. Place the PVC blisters with products into the machine mold.
3. Step on the foot pedal, the machine presses down for heat sealing.
4. The machine automatically rewinds/feeds aluminum foil.
5. Cut off the blisters with scissors or cutting machine and trim edges.
6. Get the finished sealed product.

### 5.2 Common Problems & Solutions
#### 1. Poor Blister Forming (Deformation, Perforation, Incomplete)
- Causes: Insufficient/excessive heating temperature, unstable air pressure, unparallel mold, insufficient cooling.
- Solutions: Calibrate temperature (140–160℃), stabilize air pressure 0.6–0.8MPa, adjust mold parallelism, clean air vents, optimize cooling water flow.

#### 2. Weak Heat Sealing (Leakage, Bubbles, Easy to Tear)
- Causes: Short heating time, low temperature, insufficient pressure, damaged aluminum foil adhesive layer, dirt on heating plate.
- Solutions: Extend heating time to 4–5s, increase temperature by 5–10℃, adjust pneumatic pressure, replace high‑quality aluminum foil, clean dirt on heating plate surface.

#### 3. Aluminum Foil Wrinkling & Deviation
- Causes: Uneven foil feeding tension, unparallel guide rollers, unbalanced pressure bar height, poor aluminum foil quality.
- Solutions: Adjust foil feeding tension knob, calibrate guide roller parallelism, fine‑tune pressure bar height, replace wrinkle‑free and uniform thickness aluminum foil.

#### 4. Low Production Speed & Efficiency
- Causes: Unreasonable parameter settings, slow manual feeding, poor mold adaptability.
- Solutions: Optimize heating/sealing parameters, train employees for fast feeding, customize molds with higher compatibility (max 180×140mm / 7.09×5.51in).

#### 5. Machine Cannot Start / Heating Plate Not Heating
- Causes: Poor power connection, faulty temperature controller, damaged heating tube, no air supply connected.
- Solutions: Check power circuit and plug, calibrate or replace temperature controller, repair heating tube, ensure normal air supply of air compressor and no leakage.
