## 1. Product Overview
The ETBPC-180 Blister Cutting Machine is a dedicated auxiliary device designed specifically for use with blister packing machines. Its core function is to precisely cut off the excess aluminum foil film from the edges of formed blister packs, producing neat and complete finished blisters.
It is suitable for small-batch production in pharmaceuticals, healthcare, cosmetics, small hardware, and other industries. Featuring a compact body and simple operation, it is an ideal supporting equipment for blister production lines.

## 4. Operation Steps
1. Place the semi-finished blister (sealed by the blister packing machine with excess film) into the customized machine mold.
2. Start the device; the machine will automatically press down to cut.
3. The excess film and burrs are removed instantly.
4. Retrieve the neat and intact finished blister pack.
