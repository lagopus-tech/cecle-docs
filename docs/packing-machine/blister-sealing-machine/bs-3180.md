## 4. Operation Procedure
1. The rotary table contains three stations. Operators place products into blisters on the empty station mold, fit blisters into mold grooves, then cover with paper card or Tyvek sheet.
2. Rotate the table to shift the loaded mold to the heating station for preheating.
3. Rotate the station to the sealing zone, the air cylinder descends to complete pneumatic thermal compression sealing.
4. Continue rotating the table to the unloading station; operators remove finished goods and reload materials simultaneously for cyclic operation.
5. Three sets of molds take turns for feeding, heat sealing and unloading to achieve uninterrupted continuous production.
