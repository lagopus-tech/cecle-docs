## 4. Operation Process
1. Manually place the product into the blister pack.
2. Put the blister pack with product on the mold plate and cover with paper card.
3. Manually push the mold plate into the sealing station.
4. The machine automatically presses down pneumatically to complete heat sealing.
5. After sealing, pull out the mold plate manually and take out the finished product.

A2: No, the mold is not included and needs to be customized separately.

**Q3: Is the temperature adjustable?**
