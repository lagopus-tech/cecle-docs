7. **Full Customization**: Sealing size, mold cavities, heating specifications can be customized; one machine adapts to various irregular special-shaped products.

## 4. Operation Process
1. Manually load products into blisters at the empty station, place blisters in mold grooves and cover with paper cards/Tyvek;
2. Manually rotate the turntable, transfer the loaded mold to the platen heating station for constant temperature preheating with uniform surface heating;
3. Rotate the station to the sealing area, cylinder presses down to complete pneumatic heat sealing, adapting to product edges with full-surface heating;
4. Turntable continues to rotate to the unloading station, manually take out sealed products, and load new materials at the idle station simultaneously;
5. Three sets of molds alternate for loading, preheating-sealing, and unloading, realizing seamless full-process circulation and uninterrupted production.
