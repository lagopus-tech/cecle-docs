## 4. Operation Process
1. The rotary table contains three stations. Operators place products into blisters at the empty station mold, put blisters into mold grooves, then cover with paper card or Tyvek paper.
2. Rotate the rotary table to move the loaded mold to the heating station for preheating.
3. Rotate the station to the sealing area, the air cylinder presses down to finish pneumatic thermal sealing.
4. Keep rotating the table to the unloading station; operators take out finished products and refill materials at the same time for cyclic operation.
5. Three sets of molds work alternately for feeding, heat sealing and unloading to realize uninterrupted continuous production.
