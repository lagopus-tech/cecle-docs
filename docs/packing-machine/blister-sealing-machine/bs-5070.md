## 4. Operation Process
1. The rotary table has left and right stations: manually load products into blisters at the empty station, place blisters into mold grooves, and cover with paper cards/Tyvek;
2. Manually rotate the rotary table to move the loaded mold to the heating station for preheating and insulation;
3. The station rotates to the sealing position, and the cylinder presses down to complete high-temperature pneumatic heat sealing;
4. The rotary table returns to the unloading station, finished products are manually taken out, and new products are loaded at the empty station simultaneously;
5. Two sets of molds alternate back and forth for cyclic operation, realizing uninterrupted continuous production of the machine.

### FAQs
**Q1: Does the machine come with two sets of molds when delivered?**
A1: The dual-station structure requires 2 sets of molds to achieve cyclic uninterrupted production. Molds are not included in the main machine price and are charged separately for customization.

**Q2: How to select the voltage?**
A2: Supports 110V/220V single-phase. 110V/220V can be customized for foreign trade orders.

**Q3: Can multi-cavity molds be made?**
A3: Sealing area 700*500mm (27.56×19.69inch), 1/2/3/4 cavity molds can be customized, flexibly adjusted according to product size.

**Q4: What about the equipment capacity?**
A4: Standard 10 seals per minute, suitable for low-speed precision packaging of large products, ideal for large-scale large product assembly line production.

**Q5: Is an air compressor mandatory?**
A5: The machine adopts a pneumatic pressurization structure, and an air compressor with 0.4~0.8MPa is a necessary supporting equipment.
