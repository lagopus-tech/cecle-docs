> The Desktop Vacuum Skin Packaging Machine (available in SP-220 and SP-330 models) is a compact, high-efficiency solution tailored for boutique food businesses, including supermarket counters, butcher shops, seafood retailers, and gourmet kitchens. ### 7. Workflow
1. **Tray Placement**: Place the tray containing the food into the machine's mold cavity.
2. **Film Coverage**: Drape the skin packaging film over the top of the tray.
3. **Initiate Packaging**: Close the lid; the machine automatically begins the vacuum process.
4. **Heat Sealing**: Once the vacuum is achieved, the machine automatically heats the film to bond it to the tray base and the food surface.
5. **Unloading**: Open the lid, manually trim the excess film, and remove the finished product for display.
