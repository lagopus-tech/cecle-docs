### 7. Workflow
1. **Automatic Feeding**: Manually place the trays containing food onto the workstation.
2. **Positioning Film**: The rotary table sends the tray to the skin film covering area, which is automatically applied.
3. **Vacuum Skinning**: Vacuumize and heat the film to make it tightly adhere to the food.
4. **Cutting & Sealing**: After bonding, complete the film cutting while sealing.
5. **Unloading**: After sealing, it automatically resets, and the operator manually rotates the table to remove the finished product.
