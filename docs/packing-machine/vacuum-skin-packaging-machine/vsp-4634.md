### 7. Workflow
1. **Manual Loading**: Place the trays containing food into the mold cavity in the loading area.
2. **Automatic In-feed**: Press the start button or foot switch; the mold will automatically slide into the machine.
3. **Heating & Skinning**: The top film is heated and softened, and the vacuum pump starts to remove air from the chamber, causing the film to tightly bond to the food surface.
4. **Sealing & Cutting**: After bonding, the machine performs heat sealing and trims the film around the edges for a clean finish.
5. **Unloading**: The mold slides out automatically, allowing the operator to remove the finished trays.
