## IV.  Operation & Workflow

### 1. Required Materials
Running this machine requires no air compressor or power source. Customers only need to prepare the basic consumables:
* **Screen**：Customized screens are required based on the printed patterns.
* **Ink**：Choose the appropriate type of ink based on the substrate material to be 
* **Other Consumables**：Squeegee, screen wash, thinner.

### 2. Standard Workflow
1. **Clamp the Screen**：Insert one side of the screen into the machine's metal frame clamps, tighten the screws to secure it, and adjust the counterweight.
2. **Place the Product**：Lift up the screen, place the product to be printed flat onto the table, and make positioning marks (positioning tape can be applied).
3. **Manual Printing**：Lower the screen, pour an appropriate amount of ink onto the screen, grasp the squeegee with both hands, and scrape the ink backward with even pressure.
4. **Lift Screen & Retrieve Substrate**：Lift the screen with one hand and take out the printed product to complete one printing cycle.
