## I. Product Overview

### 1. Product Category
* **Equipment Name**：Semi-automatic Vertical Flatbed Screen Printing Machine
* **Model**：TD-3050 / TD-4060 / TD-5080 / TD-60100
* **Equipment Positioning**：A vertical lifting screen printing equipment. It adopts a vertical structure where the screen frame lifts vertically and the print head moves left and right.
* **Form Factor**：**Vertical Machine**.  Its operation is extremely stable, suitable for high-intensity flatbed printing tasks in factories.

### 2. Applications
* **Industry Applications**：Widely used in screen printing for glass panels, metal plates, nameplates, paper, ceramic decals, and various flat materials.
* **Material Characteristics**：The vertical lifting structure effectively prevents ink from running and ensures stable operation. It is perfect for flat products with thicknesses up to 30mm.
* **Production Scale**：Suitable for continuous production and processing in medium to large-sized factories, with a maximum printing speed of up to 1000 times/hour.

### 3. Core Functions
* **Vertical Lifting Structure**：The screen frame lifts up and down vertically and smoothly, while the print head is driven by a variable-frequency motor to move left and right, ensuring printing accuracy and uniform coating.
* **Three-Mode Switching**：Supports automatic, semi-automatic, and manual modes, adapting to operators of different proficiency levels.
* **Vacuum Device**：The worktable is equipped with a vacuum suction device and allows for micro-adjustments in front/back and left/right directions for quick registration.

## II.  Core Advantages

* **Vertical Structure**：Vertical lifting ensures that the ink is distributed more evenly on the screen, preventing ink leakage or ghosting caused by tilting.
* **Powerful Variable-Frequency Drive**：The left and right movement of the print head is driven by a variable-frequency motor, with adjustable left/right running speeds and extremely constant squeegee pressure.
* **User-Friendly Design**：Easy installation, removal, and cleaning of the squeegee/flood bar and screen.

## IV.  Operation & Workflow

### 1. Required Auxiliary Equipment
Before operating this machine, customers need to prepare or purchase the following consumables and auxiliary equipment additionally:
* **Air Compressor**：Mandatory. The machine needs to be connected to an air compressor for operation.
* **Screen**：Customized screens are required based on the printed patterns.
* **Ink**：Inks with corresponding properties must be selected based on the material of the printed products.
* **Consumables Preparation**：Thinner, squeegee, screen wash, fixtures.

### 2. Standard Workflow
1. **Machine Setup**： Install the screen, and adjust the squeegee pressure and printing stroke.
2. **Loading**：Manually place the substrate onto the worktable.
3. **Printing**：Trigger the foot pedal switch or set the automatic cycle, and the machine completes the printing cycle.
