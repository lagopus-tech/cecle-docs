## IV.  Operation & Workflow

Before running this machine, customers need to prepare the following consumables and auxiliary equipment themselves, or purchase them additionally from our company:
### 1. Required Materials
* **Air Compressor**：Required item. The entire series consists of pneumatic equipment. Customers must provide their own air compressor with a power rating of around 2.2KW to supply the air source.
* **Screen**：Customized screens are required based on the printed patterns.
* **Fixture & Gear**：Dedicated fixtures need to be fabricated based on the product to secure its position during printing. Printing a single color does not require a gear, but multi-color registration/double-color printing requires a gear for accurate positioning.
* **Consumables**：Ink, Thinner, screen wash, Squeegee.

### 2. Standard Workflow
1. **Install the Screen**：Securely mount the screen featuring your custom design into the machine's screen frame clamp.
2. **Add Ink**：Pour an appropriate amount of mixed ink onto the screen.
3. **Place the Product**：Position the curved product to be printed onto the support rollers (using a fixture to secure it).
4. **Start Printing**：Press the start switch, and the machine will automatically complete the pattern printing.

### FAQ
* **Q:  Can this machine print multiple colors (multi-color overprinting)?**
  * **A**:  Screen printing machines are primarily designed for single-color printing. If you need to print 2 or 3 colors on the same bottle, it is possible, but you must print them in separate passes—only one color can be printed at a time. The critical prerequisite is that your bottle must have a positioning point.
* **Q: What else do I need to buy after purchasing the machine?**
  * **A**: This is a pneumatic machine, so you must purchase an air compressor. Additionally, dedicated fixtures must be custom-made by us for your specific bottles. You will also need to prepare consumables such as screens, ink, squeegees, screen wash, and thinners.
* **Q: What about warranty and after-sales services?**
