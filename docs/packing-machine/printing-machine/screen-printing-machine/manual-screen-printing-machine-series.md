# Manual Screen Printing Machine Series

###  This series covers all of our purely manual, zero-energy screen printing equipment. Based on customer printing requirements, product types, and budgets, we divide our manual screen printing equipment into three major sub-series: Manual Flat Screen Printing Table (MS/(A)MS Series), Manual Desktop Four-Color Screen Printing Machine (NS Series), and Manual Column-Type Screen Printing Machine (YH Series). We provide you with a full range of manual printing solutions, from low-cost entry-level options to professional multi-color overprinting workshops.

---

## I. Series Selection Guide
To help customers quickly match the right machine, please refer to the following core selection rules:
Printing flat surfaces, single color, small objects (wooden boards, flat fabric pieces, glass): Please choose 【1. Manual Flat Screen Printing Table (MS/(A)MS Series)】
Printing T-shirts/apparel, requiring multi-color overprinting, personal shops/limited budget: Please choose 【2. Manual Desktop Four-Color Screen Printing Machine (NS Series)】
Printing T-shirts/backpacks, multi-color overprinting, professional screen printing workshops: Please choose 【3. Manual Column-Type Screen Printing Machine (YH Series)】

## II. Operation Preparation
All machines in this series feature **purely mechanical manual operation**, require zero energy consumption, and do not need air compressors or power supplies.
* **Required Consumables**：Custom-patterned screens, inks suitable for the specific materials, squeegees, and cleaning consumables such as screen wash.


## III. Sales Tips & FAQ

### Sales Pitch & Suggestions
* **Precise Sourcing & Funneling**：When a customer sends an inquiry, your first sentence should be: " Hello! May I ask what products you are looking to print, and how many colors need to be printed? " 
**Printing flat surfaces / single color** ➡️ Focus on recommending the **MS / (A)MS Series**。
**Printing apparel / multi-color** ➡️ Focus on recommending the **NS / YH Series**。
* **Upgrade Guidance (NS to YH)**：If a customer likes the NS series but mentions that their designs are complex, require high overprinting accuracy, or involve large order volumes, be sure to recommend the YH series rotary printing machine: "The YH series features micro-registration functions and aluminum alloy platens. Although the price is higher, it saves you a massive amount of setup and alignment time, making it an essential piece of equipment for professional screen printing workshops."


### FAQ
* **Q: Do I need to plug the machine in or connect it to an air pump after purchase?**
  * **A**: Absolutely not. This is 100% purely mechanical manual equipment. You can start working right after unboxing and completing assembly.
