## IV. Workflow

### Standard Workflow
1. **Startup & Preheating**: Turn on the power and start the exhaust fan. Turn on the UV lamps one by one, observe the external ammeter, and wait approximately 3-5 minutes until the UV lamps reach a stable working state.
2. **Parameter Setting**：Adjust the conveyor belt speed (5-25m/min) and the irradiation height of the UV lampshade according to the material of the product to be cured and the characteristics of the UV ink/glue.
3. **Continuous Feeding**：Place the products printed with UV ink or applied with glue onto the input end of the Teflon mesh belt. The products will smoothly enter the curing zone along with the belt.
4. **Cooling & Collection**：Curing is complete as the product exits the output end, where it can be collected manually or by an automatic collector.
