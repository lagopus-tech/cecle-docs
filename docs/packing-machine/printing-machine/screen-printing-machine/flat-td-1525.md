## IV.  Operation & Workflow

### 1. Required Materials
Before operating this machine, customers need to prepare themselves or purchase the following consumables and auxiliary equipment from us:
* **Air Compressor**：Mandatory. It is recommended to equip one with a capacity of about 90L and a power of around 2.55KW to provide the pneumatic air source for the machine.
* **Screen**：A custom screen must be made according to the pattern to be printed.
* **Ink**：Select ink with corresponding properties based on the material of the printed product (e.g., metal, glass, etc.).
* **Thinner**：Used to adjust the viscosity of the ink to ensure smooth printing.
* **Squeegee**：Installed on the machine to scrape and press the ink.
* **Screen Wash**：Used to clean residual ink from the screen after printing.
* **Fixture**：Used to accurately secure the product on the countertop to maximize the machine's 0.01mm high-precision advantage.

### 2. Standard Workflow
1. **Install the Screen**：Securely install the custom-patterned screen into the screen frame clamps of the machine.
2. **Place the Product**：Place the flat product to be printed onto the countertop.
3. **Add Ink**：Pour an appropriate amount of mixed ink onto the screen.
4. **Start Printing**：Start via the control panel or foot switch, and the machine will automatically descend along the guide rail and complete the pattern printing.
