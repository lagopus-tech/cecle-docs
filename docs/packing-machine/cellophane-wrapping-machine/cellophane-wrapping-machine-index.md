# Selection Guide & Product Overview: Cellophane Wrapping Machine

## I. What is a Cellophane Wrapping Machine?

A cellophane wrapping machine, also known as an overwrapping machine or "cigarette box wrapper," uses BOPP film (optionally with tear tape) to provide a tight, three-dimensional wrap for rigid cuboid items.

*   **Core Value:** Moisture-proofing, anti-counterfeiting, aesthetic enhancement, and cost-saving (eliminates the need for heat shrink tunnels, preventing damage to heat-sensitive contents).
*   **Target Industries:** Widely used for cigarettes, tea boxes, cosmetics, perfume, medicine, health supplements, stationery, and small gift box packaging.

### Core Workflow
Our equipment features a **modular innovation** compared to traditional fully automatic machines, splitting the unit into two distinct parts: the **Wrapping Unit** (Front-end) and the **Folding Unit** (Back-end) for greater flexibility.

#### 1. Wrapping Unit (Front-end Process)
Handles film wrapping, cutting, and initial sealing: Manually wraps the BOPP film 360° around the product. The device automatically cuts the film and seals the cutting point, completing a semi-finished wrap.
*   **Representative Models:** `ACW-88`, `ACW-88A`

#### 2. Folding Unit (Back-end Process)
Handles side folding and sealing: Places the semi-finished box (which has been 360° wrapped) into this unit, which automatically folds, tucks, and seals the two remaining open ends to complete the 3D wrap.
*   **Representative Models:** `3DP-88`, `A3DP-88`

### Product Naming Convention
1.  **Based on the ACW-88 Wrapping Unit:**
    *   **T** = Tear tape device
    *   **F** = Film scrap collection device
    *   **D** = Dot heating device
2.  **Based on the 3DP-88 Folding Unit:**
    *   **A** = Automatic (Pneumatic drive)
    *   **H** = Heating plates (Top/Bottom heating)
    *   **D** = Dot heating device

## III. FAQ

### 1. Consumables
*   **Q1: Can I use regular PE/POF film?**
    *   **A:** No. You must use **BOPP heat-seal film**.
*   **Q2: Recommended film thickness?**
    *   **A:** 21μm - 25μm is optimal.
*   **Q3: How does the tear tape stick?**
    *   **A:** Tear tape has heat-activated glue; the machine’s tensioner feeds it onto the film, where it bonds during heat sealing.

### 2. Adjustments & Quality
*   **Q4: Why are there wrinkles?**
    *   **A:** Often due to low temperature, moving too fast, or uneven film tension.
*   **Q5: What is the difference between "Dot Sealing" and "6-Side Sealing"?**
    *   **A:** Dot sealing fixes the shape during folding (preventing slippage), while 6-side sealing provides a final overall "mirror finish" on the top and bottom.

### 3. Hardware & Selection
*   **Q6: Do I need an air compressor for pneumatic models?**
    *   **A:** Yes, a small silent, oil-free compressor (0.6-0.8MPa) is sufficient.
*   **Q7: Which models require molds?**
    *   **A:** ACW series and A3DP-88A are mold-free. 3DP-88 and industrial models usually require custom molds.
