---

## II. Core Advantages
1.  **⭐ Key Highlight: Precision Spot Sealing System**  
    For square boxes or products with large overlapping surface areas, traditional large-area heating often causes wrinkles at the film overlap. **The ACW-88D is specially equipped with a spot sealing device**, which applies heat only to the central point on the side. This design allows the film to adhere tightly to the box, effectively preventing edge curling, eliminating excess wrinkles, and resulting in a flatter, more refined, and professional finish.
2.  **High-Precision Aluminum Heating Plate:** Uses high-quality aluminum as the heating base, ensuring even heat distribution, rapid heating, and precise temperature control for smooth, damage-free large-area sealing.
3.  **Integrated Hot Wire Cutting System:** Built-in hot wire cutter for quick and neat film cutting, with automatic heat sealing at the cut edge to prevent fraying.
4.  **Tension Adjustment System:** A professional tension system allows for flexible adjustment of film feed based on film thickness, ensuring the film has a tight, "cicada-wing" texture after wrapping.
5.  **Low Maintenance:** Robust mechanical structure with a modular circuit design. Durable and easy to maintain; daily upkeep requires only a simple cleaning of the heating surfaces.

## VII. Workflow
1.  **Preheating:** Power on, set the temperatures for the main heating plate and the spot sealer, and wait 3-5 minutes to reach the set temperature.
2.  **Film Cutting:** Pull out the film according to the product size and use the hot wire device to cut it quickly.
3.  **Bottom Folding:** Place the product in the center of the film, fold the overlapping long edges, and glide it across the main heating plate to complete the bottom seal.
4.  **Side Folding & Spot Sealing:**
    *   Manually fold the swallow-tail corners on both sides.
    *   **Align the center of the folded side with the spot sealer heating head and press gently for 1-2 seconds.**
    *   The film is instantly fixed at the center point, leaving the side flat, smooth, and free of excess wrinkles.
5.  **Quality Check:** Ensure the seals are tight and the appearance is professional. The packaging process is complete.
