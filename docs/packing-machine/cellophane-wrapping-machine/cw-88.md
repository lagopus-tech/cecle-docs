### VII. Workflow
1.  **Preheating:** Power on and set the temperature controller to the desired level.
2.  **Film Positioning:** Manually pull the BOPP film to cover the positioning base on the workbench.
3.  **Primary Wrapping:** Place the box on the base, use the manual handle to complete the bottom heat seal, and the machine will automatically cut the film.
4.  **Side Folding:** Manually fold the "swallow-tail" corners at both ends of the box.
5.  **Final Sealing:** Press the folded sides against the side heating plates to complete the final packaging.
