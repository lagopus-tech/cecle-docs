# ACW-88A Cellophane Wrapping Machine (Three-Dimensional Transparent Film Wrapping Machine)

> **core Abstract:** The ACW-88A is a professional desktop semi-automatic cellophane overwrapping machine designed for rigid square boxes in the cosmetics, pharmaceutical, and food industries (e.g., perfume, medicine, and tea boxes). It utilizes cost-effective BOPP film and an integrated tear-tape device to deliver premium, moisture-proof, and anti-counterfeiting three-dimensional packaging. Featuring precise hot-wire cutting and uniform aluminum plate heating, it serves as an efficient, budget-friendly sampling and production solution for small-to-medium enterprises when paired with manual or pneumatic folding units.

## I. Product Overview
* **Product Category**: Packaging Machinery / Cellophane Wrapping Machine / Cigarette Pack Wrapping Machine
* **Application Scenarios**: Widely used for the single-box three-dimensional wrapping of cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, health products, food, stationery, and audio-visual products (such as perfume boxes, medicine boxes, tea boxes, etc.).
* **Core Function**: Utilizes BOPP film or anti-counterfeiting tear tape to wrap rigid square boxes in three dimensions, providing moisture-proof, dust-proof, and anti-counterfeiting protection while significantly enhancing product presentation and grade.

* **Professional Heating**: Equipped with **Heating by aluminum plate** to deliver flat, tight seals without damaging the packaging box.
* **Multi-functional Adjustment**: Includes a built-in film width adjustment blade and a support plate height adjustment device to flexibly adapt to various specifications.
* **User-Friendly Operation**: Controlled by light touch switches, with a structural design that is easy to maintain and clean daily.

### FAQ
* **Q: Can the machine complete the entire wrapping process on its own?**
  * A: ACW-88A is primarily responsible for film cutting and initial wrapping. For a complete and smooth six-sided folding effect, it is highly recommended to pair it with the A3DP-88 (efficient pneumatic) or 3DP-88 (economical manual) folding machine.
* **Q: Can gold tear tape (easy-open tape) be added?**
  * A: Yes, the machine comes with a built-in tear tape device, which can enhance product anti-counterfeiting and opening convenience.

## VI. Workflow
1. **Preparation Stage**: Install the BOPP film and adjust the position of the adjustment blade according to the box size.
2. **Preheating Stage**: Turn on the power and wait for the heating plate to reach the set temperature.
3. **Film Cutting & Wrapping**: Perform film cutting via the ACW-88A and wrap the film around the product.
4. **Folding & Sealing (Core Step)**: Use the A3DP-88 (pneumatic) or 3DP-88 (manual) folding machine to fold and final heat-seal both ends of the product.
5. **Finished Product Collection**: Packaging is complete, and the final product features a tight, transparent film appearance.
