### VII. Standard Workflow
1.  **Preheating:** Switch on the power and set the heating plate temperature, waiting for the system to reach the preset value.
2.  **Film Preparation:** Prepare the pre-cut transparent film based on the product dimensions.
3.  **Manual Wrapping:** Place the product in the center of the film and perform the initial manual folding.
4.  **Side Sealing:** Press the sides of the wrapped product firmly against the aluminum heating plate to seal the film instantly.
5.  **Corner Folding:** Mimic the cigarette-pack folding style for both ends, pressing against the heating plate to shape it.
6.  **Finishing:** Check the flatness of the seal and retrieve the perfectly wrapped product.
