### I. Product Overview
*   **Product Category:** Manual Cellophane Wrapping Machine / Overwrapping Machine / Clear Film Folding & Packing Machine

### VI. Standard Workflow
1.  **Turn On & Pre-heat:** Turn on the power switch, set the heating plate temperature, and wait for the system to reach the preset temperature.
2.  **Prepare the Film:** Prepare the pre-cut clear film according to the dimensions of the product to be wrapped.
3.  **Manual Wrapping:** Place the product in the center of the film and perform the initial manual wrapping and folding.
4.  **Side Sealing:** Press the wrapped product's sides firmly against the aluminum heating plate, utilizing the heat to instantly seal the film.
5.  **Corner Folding & Bottom Sealing:** Fold both ends to mimic a cigarette box style, then quickly press and shape them on the heating plate.
6.  **Finished Output:** Inspect the flatness of the seal to complete the premium packaging.
