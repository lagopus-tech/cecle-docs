## II. Production Process & Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

**1. Substrate Preset (Wrapper Preparation)**
* **Process Description:** Operators need to accurately place the pre-cut semi-circular Punjabi wrappers (radius 120mm) onto the equipment's feeding station as the starting point for automated forming.

**2. Material Pre-processing & Loading**
* **Process Description:** Pour the prepared filling (ensuring the material state matches the selected filling system) into the dedicated storage hopper at the top of the equipment to maintain a sufficient material supply.

**3. Automated Wrapper Pulling & Servo Filling**
* **Process Description:** The mechanical transfer mechanism automatically and precisely grabs a single wrapper and sends it into the core forming area. Simultaneously, the filling system dispenses a quantified amount of filling onto the center of the wrapper according to set parameters.

**4. Precision Folding & Pressing/Sealing**
* **Process Description:** The equipment automatically folds and wraps the wrapper according to a preset trajectory. This model is standardly configured for "two folds" to complete the final semi-circular precise pressing.

**5. Automatic Discharging & Output**
* **Process Description:** The formed finished product is automatically and smoothly pushed out by the pushing mechanism. It can slide directly into a collection container or smoothly connect to a downstream automated conveyor line.

## IV. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

* **Q: Can I change the product size or weight by replacing parts on a single machine?**
  * **A:** No. The mechanical stroke and forming molds of this equipment are precisely matched for a single size (28g or 32g). The dimensions cannot be adjusted or changed.
* **Q: I am just starting out and have many types of fillings. Can I share the granular and paste filling systems?**
  * **A:** Because pastes and granular materials (like potato chunks/meat) have completely different requirements for the pushing mechanism, dual-use is currently not supported. We recommend prioritizing your main core product for equipment matching.
* **Q: Does this machine require additional auxiliary equipment?**
  * **A:** Yes, the normal operation of this equipment requires pneumatic assistance. You will need to equip it with an air compressor with a power of 2.2kW or above yourself.
